FAQ

Short answers to common questions.

What is EEG?

EEG (electroencephalography) measures electrical activity in the brain. Consumer headsets detect these signals non‑invasively.

Is it safe?

EEG headsets are non‑invasive. Viovox is software in development and is not a medical device.

Which devices are supported?

We start with Muse 2 and plan to expand to more EEG devices as the product stabilizes.
