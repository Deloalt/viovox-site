✦ Early research · neural interface for your phone

A phone you'd control
with your mind.

Viovox is an early-stage research project exploring instinctive, hands-free control of a phone — fusing EEG signals from a wearable sensor with glance and micro-expression cues from the phone's camera.

Signals & commands

Natural signals in.
Phone commands out.

We're exploring an input model that fuses two streams: brain signals from a wearable EEG sensor, and glance plus micro-expressions from the phone's front camera. Below is the rough vocabulary we're playing with — how each signal maps to a command is still open research, not a promise.
